About Me

I have a competitive nature and enjoy challenging myself. I've played tennis since I was seven years old and began coaching at sixteen.

I played tennis at the collegiate level for four years where I earned the titles of Rookie of the Year, MVP, Captain and Player of the Year.

Over the past three years I've had tough but amazing experiences as a coach for High School Junior Varisty Girls Tennis. Coaching a team of thirty-four girls taught me patience, flexibility in finding solutions and the importance of using small milestones when working towards a larger goal. I believe these attributes will help me with my future in the coding world.
